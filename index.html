With a bachelor's degree in Graphic Design and a solid self-taught background in art, my career has focused on creating impactful and functional visual experiences.

My experience ranges from creating advertising and corporate graphic pieces to brand identity design and animation. At Pymes Solutions, as a web designer, I collaborate closely with entrepreneurs to enhance their digital presence, offering solutions that combine creativity and functionality.

Additionally, I specialize in user experience (UX) and interface design (UI), using tools like Figma to create intuitive and appealing digital products. My goal is to merge art and technology to develop solutions that captivate users.

Currently, I'm immersed in learning programming languages such as Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. This foray into programming allows me to expand my skills and explore new horizons in web software development.
